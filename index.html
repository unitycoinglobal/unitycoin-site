4. Smart Contract Logic

The UnityCoin smart contract is designed for maximum security and fairness:

  • No minting or additional token creation possible.
  • Tax logic is hardcoded.
  • Predefined allocations to wallets without owner privileges.
  • Internal wallet addresses are tax-exempt to prevent double taxation on strategic actions (liquidity, staking, etc).

5. Transaction Tax System

Each transaction is subject to a 6% tax, distributed as follows:

  • 2% to liquidity pool
  • 2% to staking rewards
  • 1% to marketing wallet
  • 1% permanently burned

This mechanism promotes long-term stability, rewards holders, and decreases supply over time.
